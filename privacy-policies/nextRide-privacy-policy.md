# NextRide Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-22

NextRide is an Android dashcam app that records and manages clips locally on your device.

## Data the app accesses

NextRide may access the following types of data and device features:

- camera, to record video
- microphone, only if you enable audio recording
- precise or coarse location, to show speed, coordinates, route context, and optional address details in the overlay
- motion and sensor data, to detect impact events
- user-provided vehicle plate text
- locally stored clips, subtitle sidecar files, app settings, and local diagnostic logs

## How the app uses data

NextRide uses this data to:

- record looped dashcam clips
- optionally record audio together with video
- add date, time, speed, GPS, and vehicle plate overlays to clips
- detect impacts and lock important clips
- display clips inside the in-app gallery
- let you share clips only when you explicitly use Android's share action
- save app settings and local diagnostics on the device

## Storage

New recordings are stored in app-managed storage on the device.

The app may also import older compatible clips from a previous public folder if those files already exist on the device.

Clips, overlay sidecar files, settings, and diagnostics remain on the device unless you explicitly share a clip.

## Sharing and transfers

NextRide does not upload your clips, audio, diagnostics, settings, or location history to our servers.

NextRide does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

If you use the Share action, Android will send the selected clip to the app or service you choose.

Some Android devices may use platform geocoding services to convert coordinates into a readable address string for the overlay.

## Retention and control

You can delete clips from inside the app.

Unlocked clips may also be removed automatically when the app needs to respect your configured storage limit.

You can change or clear optional settings, including the vehicle plate field, from within the app.
